Visit Us

Easy to find — off major highway US‑27. Come visit and stay at local campgrounds or RV parks. While you're nearby, check out the area!

Local Attractions

  • LaBelle Swamp Cabbage Festival — February
  • Gatorama — Learn and play with alligators and crocodiles (Open Thurs–Mon, 10am–5pm)
  • Blueberry U‑Pick — March through May
  • Indian Mounds

South Central Florida Highlights

South Central Florida is a major producer of sugarcane. Explore:

  • Clewiston Sugar Festival — March
  • Sugarcane Moonshine Distillery — Lake Placid, FL (beautiful city of lakes)
  • Ortona Cane Grinding Festival — Indian Mounds Preserve, first Saturday in February

Outdoor Adventures

In addition to National Parks like the Everglades and State Parks in the Keys, the Florida Wildlife Corridor includes public lands for hiking, paddling, and camping. Our farm is near Fisheating Creek and Gatorama.
